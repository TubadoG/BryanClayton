To Switch quickly between applications, (i.e. internet to Finale) Press Command-Tab on a Mac and Alt-Tab on a PC. Continue to hold Command or Tab and tap the tab buttton to cycle through all open applications.

8.5 x 11 Flashcards

This tutorial will walk you through the process to create 8.5x11 flashcards for your classroom. In this tutorial, you will use these skills:

  1. The Launch Window
  2. Changing Views and Page orientation
  3. Resizing with the Resize Tool
  4. Hide and show items with the Staff Tool
  5. Moving notes with the Special Tools
  6. Label with the Lyric Tool
  7. Setting up Finale to print in landscape
