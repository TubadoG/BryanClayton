Page 3 - Resizing

For our note name flash cards, we want to have one big measure so your students will be able to see from around the room. Our first step is to get rid of all the extra measures after measure one. There are lots of different ways to do this but most require the same tool. We want to edit a large region of the score... Find the tool from the tool menu that seems like it will allow you to edit...

I guess this was another easy one. If you selected the Mass Edit tool, you are correct. Now click on measure two, then hold down the shift key, last, press the right arrow. You will see all of the extra measures highlighted now. To get rid of them, press the delete or backspace key on your keyboard.

Next, we need to make it really big! In the Tools menu, find the tool that will change the size of an object...

Again, you probably guessed it! If you found the Resize tool, you found the correct tool. Now its time to tell Finale what you want to Resize. This can be tricky depending on where you click. You can click on a note head, a staff system, or the corner of the page. These three clicks will all change the size of something different. To change the size of a measure of music, where should I click??? You guessed it, on the staff system. Feel free to experiment with the different sizes in the Staff Size dialogue box. FOr the purposes of this tutorial, we will use 999%, that's as big as you can make it.

If you don't think that's big enough, we can also resize the entire page by clicking on the corner of the page. Try that a few times until you get a staff and page size that you think is large enough. When you are ready to go to the next page, click below.
