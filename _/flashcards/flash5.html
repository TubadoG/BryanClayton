Inserting Notes

Select the Simple Entry tool from the tools menu. There are other ways that you may know to enter music but this entry is the easiest way for this application. For this tutorial, we will use quarter notes but you may choose to use other rhythmic values if you wish. Using the simple entry pallet, select quarter notes. Now simply click a staff line or space to add the note. It might appear in purple and not in the center of the measure. That's the next thing to fix!

We are going to use the Special Tools for this operation. Find Tools->Special Tools->Note Position.

At the top of the page, you should see some boxes, or handles. Select the first box and drag the note until it is in the center of the measure. If you see gray rests in the measure, you can ignore them. because they will not print. If you don't see the boxes at the top of the page, click in the measure.

When you have a note in your measure, you are ready to go on to the next page.
